I am from Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a beautiful country located in South Asia, bordered by India, Myanmar, and the Bay of Bengal. It is known for its lush green landscapes, numerous rivers, and fertile plains that support agriculture. The country has a rich cultural heritage, including traditional music, dance, festivals, and cuisine. People of Bangladesh are known for their hospitality, resilience, and hardworking nature. Dhaka, the capital city, is a bustling metropolis with a mix of modern and historical landmarks. Bangladesh also has a growing economy, thriving textile industry, and a commitment to education and technological development. Tourists are attracted to its natural beauty, historical sites, and vibrant traditions.
